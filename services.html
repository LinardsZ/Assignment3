Services

Baltik Bright Cleaners offers many home and commercial cleaning services for you.

Window Cleaning

If you’re noticing your windows look a bit dull, we can come by and do a clean up. It’s about getting rid of marks and dirt so you can see out better. We wipe down the glass, take care of any spots, and do our best to make them shine a bit.

Deep Cleaning

When you think it's time for a serious cleanup, call us. We go all out, cleaning stuff that's usually skipped during regular clean-ups. It’s like a big, all-over clean that gets your place ready for any big day or just because you feel like it. Our crew gets into all those hard spots and gives everything a good wipe down.

Move-in / Move-out Cleaning

Moving can be a hassle, but the cleaning doesn’t have to be. Whether you’re leaving an old place or starting fresh somewhere new, we’re here to sweep, scrub, and wipe everything down. It’s pretty thorough so that you can move on to setting up your new space without old dirt or fuss about the one you're leaving.

Daily Chores

Everyone needs a hand with daily stuff around the house, right? We can take care of your regular cleaning chores like doing the laundry, washing dishes, or sweeping up your floors. It’s just about giving you more time to relax instead of worrying about these everyday tasks. Let us know what you’re struggling to keep up with, and we'll sort it out.

Custom Cleaning

Got something specific in mind? Whether it’s a certain room that’s hard to tackle or some items that need careful cleaning, tell us what the problem is, and we’ll make a plan. We clean different things based on what you say you need. It’s all quite flexible.

Disinfecting Services

Sometimes, a normal clean just isn’t enough, especially if you’re worried about germs. Our extra service focuses on making your place not just look clean, but also be more germ-free. We pay extra attention to the spots that get a lot of hand-touches, using stronger stuff to kill germs and keep everything super sanitary.
